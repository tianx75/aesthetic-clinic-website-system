## Quick Replace Workflow

1. Open all HTML files in your code editor
2. Use **Find in Files** (Cmd+Shift+F / Ctrl+Shift+F)
3. Replace in this order:
   - `Aesthetic Clinic` → Your clinic name (catches most instances)
   - `info@yourclinic.com` → Your email
   - `+1 (555) 000-0000` → Your phone
   - `City` → Your city (be careful — use "Match Whole Word" to avoid replacing "City" inside other words)
   - `yourclinic.com` → Your domain
   - `NOVA Pro` → Your device name (replace BEFORE "NOVA" alone)
   - `NOVA` → Your treatment line
4. Review each page visually
5. Update meta descriptions with your specific keywords
