Program Tiers

Three tiers.
One philosophy.

Every tier serves the same goal: informed, conscious decisions in aesthetic medicine.

01
Tier
B2C · Paid

Informed Patient Workshop

For individuals seeking clarity before committing to treatment.

2–3 hour workshop (online + in-person), 2–4 sessions per month

  • Aesthetic anatomy fundamentals, indications vs. contraindications
  • Treatment types (NOVA, injectables) and expected outcomes
  • Risks, recovery timelines, and realistic expectations
  • Mini consultation: what to ask, what to look for
$250–$450 per person

50% of the workshop fee is credited toward your first treatment within 14 days.

Register
02
Tier
B2B · Professional · Paid

Associate Certification Program

For consultants and staff from other clinics.

2 days theory + 1 day hands-on + exam (quarterly cohorts, max. 16 participants)

  • Consultation framework: needs assessment, goals, recognizing contraindications
  • Ethics, legal compliance, GDPR; when to escalate to a physician
  • Sales communication without pressure (premium brand voice)
  • Situational exercises, role-play, and shadowing
$1,500–$3,000 per person

Aesthetic Clinic Associate certificate and 12-month digital badge.

Apply Now
03
Tier
Internal · Invitation Only

In-House Associate Training

For top candidates selected from Tier 2.

4–6 week onboarding + 3-month mentored period

  • Workflow: systems, protocols, physician escalation procedures
  • KPI-driven operations, daily standups, case library
  • Minimum 12-month collaboration agreement

This tier is not public — by invitation only.
