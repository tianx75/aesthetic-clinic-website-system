Synergistic Mechanisms

How the two treatments enhance each other

01
Collagen Matrix Stabilisation
Photobiomodulation stimulates fibroblasts, which produce new collagen fibres around the filler. This forms a structural network that reduces migration risk and delivers more durable results.
02
Accelerated Recovery
LED light therapy's anti-inflammatory and microcirculation-enhancing effects reduce post-injection oedema and bruising, accelerating the recovery process.
03
Muscle Tone Restoration
The NOVA Pro EMS component activates facial muscles and improves contours — particularly in the jawline, midface and mandibular region. Volume restoration alone cannot achieve this.
04
More Natural Results
NOVA Pro tightens tissues and activates muscles, while the filler restores lost volume. Together, they produce dynamic, natural facial rejuvenation — not an "overfilled" appearance.
