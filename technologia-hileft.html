Neuromodulators & NOVA Pro:
Why They Complement
Each Other

At first glance, it seems paradoxical: neuromodulators relax the muscles, while EMS stimulates them. In reality, these technologies work not against each other, but in concert.

Neuromodulators block neuromuscular transmission — which over time can lead to muscle thinning (disuse atrophy). NOVA Pro technology uses a "technical bypass" to directly stimulate the muscle fibers, preserving their mass and structural integrity.

The result: the muscle retains its strength and tone — but mimetic movements do not return. A denser collagen network ensures longer-lasting aesthetic results.

"The question is no longer how to eliminate wrinkles, but how to optimize cellular function non-invasively — by activating the body's own biological reserves."
