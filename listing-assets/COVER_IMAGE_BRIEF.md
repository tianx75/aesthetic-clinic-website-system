## Mockups

- **Desktop:** `screenshots/homepage-hero.png` inside a minimal light-gray browser chrome frame, angled 2–3° for depth
- **Mobile:** `screenshots/mobile-preview.png` inside a dark iPhone frame, overlapping the bottom-right of the desktop mockup

## Do NOT Include

- Price
- "Sale" or "discount" text
- Cluttered backgrounds
- Stock photos behind the mockups
- Gradients that compete with the product screenshots

## Recommended Tools

- **Canva:** Start from blank 1600×900, add mockup frames from Elements
- **Figma:** Use free browser/phone mockup components from Community
- **Smartmockups.com:** Upload screenshots into device frames
