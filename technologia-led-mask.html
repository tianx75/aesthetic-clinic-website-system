The Protocol extends beyond the clinic

Not an accessory.
Part of the system.

The effects of most aesthetic treatments end at the clinic door. In the NOVA system they do not — because the LED Mask is the integral third pillar of the Prepare–Treat–Recover protocol.

The mask does not replace clinical intervention. It extends it. Daily 10–20 minute at-home photobiomodulation maintains cellular-level activation between treatments — ensuring the biological response is never interrupted.

Why does this matter?
Collagen neogenesis is not a single event — it is a process. If stimulation is limited to the treatment session alone, the tissue response becomes inconsistent. The LED Mask provides continuous, low-intensity maintenance — so the biological adaptation remains unbroken.
